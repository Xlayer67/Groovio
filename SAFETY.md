# Groovio Safety & Community Rules

Groovio is a teen-safe music platform designed for creativity, learning, and positive expression.

## 1. Age Safety
- Groovio is designed for users aged 13+
- Accounts for users under 13 are restricted
- No adult-only content is allowed

## 2. Content Rules
The following content is NOT allowed:
- Sexual or explicit content
- Violence or gore
- Drugs, alcohol, or illegal activity
- Hate speech, bullying, or harassment
- Dangerous challenges or self-harm content

## 3. Music & Media Rules
- Only upload original content or content you have rights to
- No copyrighted music uploads without permission
- Remixes must follow Groovio remix rules

## 4. Groovey AI Assistant Rules
Groovey is a music-focused AI coach.
- Groovey helps with music, rhythm, and learning
- Groovey does NOT provide:
  - Sexual content
  - Dangerous advice
  - Illegal instructions
- Unsafe questions are politely refused or redirected

## 5. Moderation
- AI + human moderation is used
- Users can report content
- Admins may warn, suspend, or ban accounts
- Repeat violations result in permanent bans

## 6. Economy Safety
- G-Bux cannot be used to buy real-world items
- No scams or fake giveaways
- Anti-cheat systems are enforced

## 7. Community Respect
- Be kind and respectful
- No impersonation
- No spam or abuse
